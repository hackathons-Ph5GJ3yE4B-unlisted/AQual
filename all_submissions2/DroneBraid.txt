Inspiration ELC kickoff, the speaker wrote about how no one has been able to figure out the solution to tying a good ponytail for the disabled community. I spent a few hours watching youtube videos on how to tie a ponytail with one arm. I tried it myself. Brought almost to tears with frustration. Knew there needed to be a solution.

What it does Four drones use computer vision, AI learning, gyroscopic flight, and gentle hair gathering claws to deliver simple updo's.
