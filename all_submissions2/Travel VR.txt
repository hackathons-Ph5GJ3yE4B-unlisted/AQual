The technology can prove to be very useful for differently abled people, for whom travel to such destinations might not be possible.
Some use cases can for travel VR for specially abled can be-
• People with schizophrenia disorder, uncertainty and bewilderment can be triggered when the person witnesses unfamiliar airport crowd, general excitement and flying in an airplane. This arousal in fear can result in full-blown delusion and seizures in many cases. A travel VR equipment can prove to be of critical importance by dimming the surroundings hence making travel possible for these individuals without the need of being sedated.
• An individual who is handicapped may not always find possible to travel to different location. With a travel VR headset, the individual can visit the popular destinations and even get a bird’s eye view of the said place. Technology can also be developed which will enable a real time connection between two different individuals and the VR user can tap into a live feed captured by his partner at the location.
• Enhanced experience systems can be developed with the VR system, for example smart shoes, which can further enhance the user experience using haptic feedback via vibrations further making the experience more lifelike.
• VR tech along with smart add-ons can also be used for a multitude of areas apart for tourism, for example the technology can help the especially abled to attend business/corporate meetings without need to be present physically.
