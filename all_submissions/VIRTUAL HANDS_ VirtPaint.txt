WHAT IS IT?

«VIRTUAL HANDS: VirtPaint» - support VR Application for special people, which offers an outstanding opportunity for hundreds of thousands of people around the globe, who don’t have the use of physical arms, to paint and see their paintings come to life.

This App is initially designed specially for people with disabilities, who encounter many more problems than other people do in life. Developers team are working hard to change this and give an exclusive opportunity for these important members of our society, to feel unlimited possibilities to draw as an artist with virtual paints, canvas and brush.
That gives you a completely fresh, never felt before experience of painting in a virtual reality world, using only head movements.

App developers are literally giving Virtual Hands to those people, so they can express and enjoy themselves, satisfy the desire to create. VirtPaint is a new, interesting and relaxing experience.

Our goal is to create series of projects for those amongst us who don’t have the use of physical arms. We could help them with Gear VR and a series of our Support Applications «VIRTUAL HANDS: VirtPaint» : VirtMusic, VirtArchitecture, VirtReader, etc.
You need VR headset to test App «VirtPaint».

Hands free, no need to use touchpad or controller. Head tracking only.

1 Point on object and wait for few sec.

2 Choose the brush and wait for few sec.

3 Choose the color and wait for few sec.

4 Create!
